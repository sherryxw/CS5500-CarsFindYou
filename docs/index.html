Introduction

Buying a car can be a laborious task. It takes time and effort to search for cars and negotiate with dealers. We make this process more efficient by introducing this car trading website. Unlike other websites where buyers find dealers, people can post their demands and wait for dealers to match the posts.

Features

  • New users register either as a buyer or a dealer.
  • Buyers post their demands and specify the configurations.
  • Dealers search buyers’ posts and make an offer if it’s a good match.
  • Buyers can accept/decline offers. Dealers can withdraw their own offers.
